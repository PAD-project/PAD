Extra information about us

Welcome to the capture the flag challenge of Masters of Deception.
On this webiste you can challenge your hacking skills and knowledge about Broken Authentication.
In the navigation bar on the left you can find the challenges and some more information.

School Project

This product is created from a school project. The aim of our project is to create a product which users can broaden their knowledge on security threats. our product foccuses on the Broken Authentication aspect, where users need to find the correct login credentials. The other aspect of this project is to introduce its students to the SCRUM project method. Most of us students are new to this method and have not used it in our previous project (FYS).
