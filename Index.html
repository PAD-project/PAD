Introduction

Welcome to the capture the flag challenge of Masters of Deception. On this webiste you can challenge your hacking skills and knowledge about Broken Authentication.

In the navigation bar on the left you can find the challenges and some more information.

NOTICE: You will encouter situations where you maybe don't know what to do anymore. DONT PANIC, some situations are difficult. There are no time limits so maybe just take break get some fresh air and return when you're ready.

If you find bugs or maybe have some new suggestions for new challenge ideas, let us know! we appreciate the feedback.

For now go get hacking and test your skills!
