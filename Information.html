History

This concept is part of our project assignment. But who are we? Our names are: Max Klaver, Teun Bugera, Daan Huiberts, Hasan Konuksever and Sameer Butt and originialy we are projectgroup IC103A-1. We study HBO ICT Cyber Security at the HVA in Amsterdam.

Our project is to create a capture the flag challenge for users to test their knowledge and skills in hacking games. Our games are inspired by the OWASP top 10 list of known security threats and the games that we developed are about the threat Broken Authentication.
